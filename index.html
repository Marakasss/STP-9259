About the game

Enter an era of war, betrayal, and resilience in Dynasty: Fallen & Rise, a tactical strategy RPG where your choices shape the fate of an empire.

Play as an exiled heir fighting to reclaim the throne of your fallen kingdom. Build alliances, master battlefield tactics, and command legendary heroes in your quest to unite the land once more.

Craft your legacy in a world where every sword stroke echoes through history.

  • Rebuild your dynasty from ruin.
  • Command armies in real-time battles.
  • Outsmart rivals in political intrigue and strategy.

How to play

  • 1

    Choose Your Faction – Each dynasty comes with unique strengths, histories, and heroes.

  • 2

    Recruit Legendary Heroes – Assemble a diverse warband of generals, assassins, and sages.

  • 3

    Build & Expand – Restore your city, train your army, and expand across war-torn lands.

  • 4

    Conquer Territories – Engage in tactical, turn-based combat and siege enemy strongholds.

  • 5

    Form Alliances – Team up with other players or betray them for power.

  • 6

    Shape Your Legacy – Every decision impacts your dynasty’s rise… or fall.

Game features

  • Deep Strategic Combat: Tactical battles that challenge your mind and military precision.

  • Massive Campaign Map: Explore kingdoms, mountains, deserts, and lost temples.

  • Hero System: Recruit and train legendary warriors, each with their own backstory and skills.

  • Interactive Storytelling: Choices affect relationships, war outcomes, and future events.

  • Dynasty Management: Manage resources, diplomacy, armies, and political stability.

  • Stunning Visuals: Breathtaking animations, hand-drawn portraits, and dynamic environments.

  • PvP & PvE Modes: Compete globally or conquer through immersive solo campaigns.

FAQ

  • Is Dynasty: Fallen & Rise free to play?

    Yes! You can play for free, with optional in-app purchases for cosmetics and boosts.

  • Can I play offline?

    Core story content is available offline, though multiplayer modes require an internet connection.

  • Are there different endings?

    Yes! Player choices during campaigns influence the fate of characters and kingdoms.

  • Is there a multiplayer mode?

    Yes! Participate in real-time PvP battles and join alliances to fight in kingdom-scale wars.

  • Will new heroes be added?

    Absolutely. New content, heroes, and campaigns are regularly introduced via updates.
